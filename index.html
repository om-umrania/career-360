What about the weekly data reporting?
Sakhi

Recorded marketplace and product data, then presented it in weekly and monthly reviews — week-on-week movement, retention snapshots, first- and second-order cuts of the underlying datasets to surface patterns worth discussing. I was the reporter, not the decider.

chain-of-thought · weekly review notes
WhatsApp messaging — what changed?
Sakhi

Ran a longitudinal journey for 150–200 users across different products over a 12-week window. The discovery was about engagement rate — where people actually stay vs. drop off. That changed how we drafted outbound: bold / italics for scannable emphasis, a tighter emoji system, and the rule of one key message per image.

reasoning · 12-week longitudinal cohort
Anything on the Meta / OmniVec-ICMR side?
Sakhi

For OmniVec-ICMR, proposed a sample-data dashboard for the campaign — kept the claims to what was actually built, not the downstream reach. Bigger thing there was the Saathealth Language Translation chatbot: built as a Gemini Gem trained on how Varun frames outbound — the humour, the Saathealth positioning, the voice — so translations across 7 Indian languages retain brand tone rather than reading machine-flat.
